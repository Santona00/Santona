Design, Development, and Evaluation of VIRTRON: a Virtual Try-on Application :
This article presents the development of a Pythonbased 2D virtual try-on system prototype that leverages realtime video input from a 60fps webcam. The system utilizes the MediaPipe library to track 33 body coordinates, enabling accurate body tracking in real-time. By measuring clothing garments' dimensions and the user's left and right shoulder and hip coordinates, the system calculates the aspect ratio and overlays the dress onto the body. Furthermore, a Flask API is implemented to connect the virtual try-on system with a web app, enhancing the user experience. This article highlights the innovative features and potential applications of the virtual try-on system, showcasing its ability to revolutionize the fashion industry by providing an immersive and realistic shopping experience. Lastly, usability evaluation and SUS evaluation are done to evaluate the system.
